    Chloe, all dressed up

    2019 Christmas gymkhana

     

    Week One Classes

    Saturday Classes Gymkhana is the 7th December           

    Sunday Classes Gymkhana is the 8th December

     

    Week Two Classes

    Saturday Classes Gymkhana is the 14th December         

    Sunday Classes Gymkhana is the 15th December

     

     

    When?

     

    Each class has their very own Gymkhana held at their normal class time on their last lesson for the year.  The Gymkhana runs for 4 hours (as per normal class). 

    Family members are more than welcome.

     

     

    What is a Gymkhana?

     

    A gymkhana is a mixture of all different competition events showing the skills that our students have learnt thoughout the year.  The students will not be asked to do anything they haven't done before in their lessons.

     

     

    Does my child need to bring anything?

     

    A plate of Christmas party food to share.

    Dress as normal and try to arrive on time as the events begin at the start time of the lesson.

     

     

    Cost?

     

    No extra charge

     

     

    Awards and Ribbons?

     

    A presentation of awards will be held at the end of each Gymkhana.
