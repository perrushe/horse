    PROGRAMS

     

    We Specialise in Teaching Children how to Horse

    Ride & Care for Horses

     

    **  NEW DAY OFFERED THURSDAY 9TH JULY **

     

    "OWN A HORSE FOR A DAY"

      

     During these Fun Filled and Instructive days the students will ....

    • Catch the Horses and Bring them into the stables
    • Learn to Groom, Saddle and Prepare their Horse to be Ridden
    • Learn how to look after their very own horse.
    • Lots of Hands On activities!  How to Wash a Horse, Prepare a Horse for a Show, Grooming, Feeding and Rugging.
    • Includes : One Hour Lesson in the Olympic Size Indoor Arena
    • Includes : One Hour Bush Ride around our Beautiful Property or Games on Horseback.
    • Lots and Lots of time to enjoy the Horses and Ponies

     

    Time  :  9:30 am - 3:30 pm (6 Hour Session)   

    Cost  :  $125.00 per Rider   (BYO Lunch)

     

    Dates :   JULY 2020 DATES 

                  NEW DATES: Wednesday 8th and Thursday 9th July

                  Wednesday 1st July and Tuesday 7th July (more dates will become available)

     

    Most Horse Handling and Riding is conducted in a fully undercover / enclosed complex. 

    These sessions are for Boys & Girls aged between 8 and 16 years old, for Beginner and Intermediate riders
