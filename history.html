    • OUR History ....

    Melbourne Indoor Equestrian Centre is owned and operated by Ross Little and Leisa Zaninelli (NCAS/AHRC Level One).

     

    We have been providing quality Horse Riding Lessons for Children and Horse Agistment in Mebourne since 1997.

     

    Melbourne Indoor Equestrian Centre is situated on the outer-northern fringes of Melbourne.

    We are close to one hours easy drive from melbourne CBD in Kinglake West on 92 acres, and offers first class purpose built facilities.

     

    Students are able to ride in a safe, fun and friendly atmosphere.  All activities are fully supervised by kowledgable, professional and friendly staff.

     

    We own all our our experienced and very trustworthy horses and ponies.
