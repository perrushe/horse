    Welcome to Melbourne Indoor

    Equestrian Centre

     

    At Melbourne Indoor Equestrian Centre we offer a full range of Horse Riding activities for Children on our Horses & Ponies.

     

    OLYMPIC SIZED INDOOR ARENA AND UNDERCOVER STABLE COMPLEX

     

     DRESSAGE - SHOWJUMPING - TRAIL RIDING - HORSE HANDLING - X-COUNTRY - GAMES

     

    Learn to Horse Ride Safely with Professional Children’s Horse Riding Tuition

    • A Complete Horse Riding Experience in a club atmosphere,  Close to Melbourne and Specialising in Children’s Horse Riding Lessons!

    Our magnificent facilities include an Olympic Size Indoor Arena with adjoining 21 stable complex, Outdoor Arena and Trails throughout our picturesque 92 Acre Equestrian Property.

     

    We also have our Parents Retreat Room for those parents wishing to stay and watch their children horse riding lesson.

     

     

     

     

    Melbourne Indoor Equestrian Centre Weekend Horse Riding Club

    Learn All aspects of Horse Riding and Caring for Horses.

     

    Along with Show Jumping, Dressage, Trail Riding and Horse Handling.

     

    Beginner to Advanced Horse Riders Catered For

     

    Safe and Reliable Horses & Ponies provided

     

    Our Team of Professionals includes Leisa Zaninelli

    Qualified Level 1 AHRC/NCAS

    Australian Horse Riding Centre Accredited Coach

    National Coaching Accreditation Scheme (NCAS)
