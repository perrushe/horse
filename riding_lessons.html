    oUR HORSES

    FORTNIGHTLY

    Our Horse Riding Club is held each fortnight and students learn all about horses and horse riding.

    The children have a formal lesson in the Indoor Arena and then another ride out on our magnificent property

    .

    Each lesson involves aprox 2 Hours Riding time !!

     

    Throughout this session all aspects of horse handling and caring for a horse is taught and experienced. This is the perfect session for all the Horse Lovers that want to learn all about horses.

     

    All activities are fully supervised by our very experienced and friendly staff

    Horse Riding

    All aspects of horse riding are taught, including:

    • Dressage
    • Showjumping
    • Bareback Horse Riding
    • Horse Riding Competitions
    • Obstacle Courses
    • Trail Rides
    • Cross Country
    • Games On Horses

    Learn How to:

    • Catch a Horse
    • Grooming & Getting Ready to Ride
    • Saddling & Bridling
    • Feeding & Rugging
    • Health of a Horse
    • Washing & Plaiting
    • Show Preparation

     

    Each member of Melbourne Indoor Equestrian Centre Horse Riding Club has their own theory book which outlines different levels of Horse Mastership, both practical and theory.

     

    As each level is obtained a certificate is awarded. At the end of each year we hold our Annual Gymkhana which is great fun for all, with trophies and ribbons being awarded to all riders.
